About Me

Hi! My name's Ryan, and I'm 28-years old writer and product manager living in the beautiful town of Yardley, Pennsylvania in Bucks County.

I lived overseas from the age of 3 to 14 in Bangladesh, Kazakhstan, and Israel. While living abroad, I spent a good bit of time at the American International School.

For high school, I followed my big brother's path of attending Phillips Exeter Academy. My big accomplishment during those four years was not quiting and learning to expand my mind with peers.

Despite being an average high school student at Exeter, I somehow found my way to the US Air Force Academy. I spent four years there and intiially wanted to study Electrical Engineering.

Ultimately, I took an easier route and studied Economics and delved into the field of Model United Nations. Also, in college, I published my first article (link: )

Now, I am trying to live a useful life and build useful things for the USAFA alumni community. If you have ideas on what I should build, you can contact me here (link:) !
